Trisha's Recipe for Truffle Pasta

Ingredients

    For the pasta:

    • 8 oz. pasta (such as tagliatelle or fettuccine)
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • Salt, to taste

    For the truffle sauce:

    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 cup heavy cream
    • 2 tablespoons truffle oil
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)
    • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the butter to the skillet and let it melt. Pour in the heavy cream and truffle oil. Stir well to combine.
  4. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss until evenly coated with the truffle sauce.
  6. Remove from heat and serve the truffle pasta immediately.
  7. Optional: Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley before serving.
